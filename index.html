AP Dhillon

AP Dhillon or Amritpal Singh Dhillon is an Indian-Canadian singer, songwriter, and music producer. Born and raised in Punjab, India, Dhillon moved to Canada at a young age and embarked on a successful music career that has garnered him a massive following worldwide. His unique blend of Punjabi folk and modern hip-hop influences has made him a rising star in the music industry.

Journey

AP Dhillon was born into a Sikh family on 10 January 1993 in Gurdaspur, Punjab, India. He studied from the Little Flower Convent School and completed his graduation in civil engineering from Baba Kuma Singh Ji Engineering College, Hoshiar Nagar affiliated under Punjab Technical University in Amritsar. Dhillon pursued a diploma in business administration and management from Camosun College in British Columbia. He has since been based in Victoria while working for Run-Up Records.

At a young age, Dhillon moved to Canada with his family, seeking better opportunities and a new beginning. It was during this transition that he discovered his passion for music and decided to pursue it as a career. Determined to make his mark in the music industry, Dhillon embarked on a journey of self-discovery and honing his craft.

My views

"AP Dhillon has swiftly captured a special place in my heart, and his music resonates deeply not just with me but also with all of my friends. Whenever the familiar tunes of Excuses or Brown Munde fill the air, it's as if a magical spell takes over, and everyone around starts singing and swaying in sync. While AP Dhillon deserves immense credit for his talents, it's important to acknowledge the incredible contributions of his partners, Shinda Kahlon and Gurinder Gill, who are the true superstars behind the captivating beats of his music. Together, these three artists represent the bright future of the Punjabi hip-hop industry. Let's unite in our support for them and embrace the musical journey they are embarking upon."
